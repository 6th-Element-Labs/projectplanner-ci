Strategy POV · why this framing

This isn't a login problem — it's a go-live problem. Every day without SSO is a day TotalEnergies can't onboard.

Audience insight

Pilot operators are field-first and low-patience — anything with friction gets worked around, which is exactly how manual provisioning crept in.

Competitive teardown
Okta-only shops — fast, but a dead end for the Entra-native buyer.
Full IdP rebuilds — "right", but months long; miss the window entirely.
Our lane — Entra-native with Okta as a safety net, proven at parity. Fast and safe.
Why now

The TotalEnergies contract has a go-live date. Inside the window it's a flagship reference; outside it's a stalled pilot.
