The challenge

Northwind's pilot operators couldn't sign in to the ops portal — every account was provisioned by hand, and that was the single thing blocking go-live with TotalEnergies.

The fix was bounded and known. The risk was shipping it late, or shipping it unproven.

The approach · Parity-First

We led with the CI-parity gate — running it from week one, alongside the build, so go-live was de-risked before the work was even finished. Entra/SAML sign-in, directory auto-provisioning, and Okta kept as a silent fallback.
